Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • About 3/4 cup of water
  • medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup of finely grated cabbage
  • 1/2 cup of finely grated carrots
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough
    • Mix the flour; add the salt.
    • Gradually add water; knead to a firm dough.
  2. Prepare the filling
    • Combine all filling ingredients; mix thoroughly by hand.
    • Ensure no lumps; make sure it's a uniform mixture.
  3. Fill the momos and steam
    • Place a spoonful of filling; gently press to form a crease.
    • Grease your steamer basket; place the momos inside.
    • Bring water to a boil; place the steamer basket over it.
