RECIPES

>> Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 (6 oz) can of tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup of water or vegetable/chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 pound (16 ounces) of dry spaghetti pasta

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Sauce
    • Heat the olive oil in a medium saucepan or pot over medium heat.
    • Add the chopped onion and cook until it becomes soft and translucent, about 5-7 minutes.
  2. Cook the Spaghetti
    • Add the dry spaghetti to the boiling water and stir it well to prevent the noodles from sticking together.
    • Cook according to the package directions, or until the spaghetti is al dente (tender but firm to the bite).
  3. Combine and serve
    • Add the drained pasta; toss with the sauce.
    • Garnish with cheese; add fresh herbs.
